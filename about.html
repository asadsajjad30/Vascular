INTRODUCTION

DVES

Since the year of 2016, the department of vascular and endovascular surgery (DVES) at SMBBIT Karachi, is dedicated to saving the lives and limbs of patients that are debilitated by complex vascular conditions.
We have come a long way since then/ We now proudly stand at the forefront of vascular care, providing free of cost services including modern vascular testing, traditional surgical interventions and minimally invasive endovascular procedures to patients from all across Pakistan.
